Arrival & Departure

Our Front Desk Team is here to welcome you and ensure a smooth process for check-in at 3:00 PM and check-out at 12:00 PM , while catering to your preferences throughout your stay.

Please note:

Upon check-in, ensure that you are holding the passports or Emirates IDs for all your guests, as this is a legal requirement in the UAE. Kindly share your estimated arrival time to help us ensure a smooth check-in experience. Meeting Rooms

Dahlia Ballroom

Square Meters: 998
The Dahlia Ballroom presents a striking backdrop for unique celebrations with its impressive mirrors reflecting a beautiful scenery. The 1,080 sq. m. can host up to 1,000 guests and it is dividable into 6 partitions. Fahlia Ballroom is considered to be one of the largest event spaces in the Heart of Fujairah

Sidr

Boardroom offers 42 sq. m. of space making it ideal for smaller meetings. It can accommodate up to 14 people in a boardroom fixed set-up.

Board Room (Siji)

Explore Fujairah

Offers a wide range of child-friendly activities, making it an ideal destination for a fun-filled family day out.
Here are some top picks to explore:

Fujairah Adventure Park

Discover Fujairah Adventure Park, the premier spot in the UAE for adrenaline-packed activities, family fun, and breath taking natural scenery.

Timings: Weekly - 3:00 PM- 11:00 PM

Seven Summits Trail

7-summits/Al Bidya hike is a combination of two entry-level hikes, ideal for beginners and families with children.

Timings: Weekly- 24/7 open

Sheikh Bin Zayed Mosque

The Sheikh Zayed Mosque, Fujairah, is the main mosque in the Emirate of Fujairah, and the second largest in the U.A.E. after the mosque with the same name in Abu Dhabi.

Timings: Weekly - 10:00 am - 8:00 PM

Fujairah Fort / Museum

One of the most important museums in the UAE with focus on Emirati history and heritage.

Timings:
8:30 AM to 1:30 PM
4:30 PM to 6:30 PM (on Fridays)
2:30 PM to 6:30 PM (on Saturdays)

Umbrella Beach

Umbrella Beach is envisioned to be the ultimate beach destination on the UAE's east coast. The beach boasts sparkling sand and clear waters, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and building sandcastles.

Timings: 24/7 Open
Swimming till 12:00 AM
