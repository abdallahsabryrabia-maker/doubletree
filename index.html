Important Information

Arrival & Departure

Our Front Desk Team is here to welcome you and ensure a smooth process for check-in at 3:00 PM and check-out at 12:00 PM , while catering to your preferences throughout your stay.

Please note:

Upon check-in, ensure that you are holding the passports or Emirates IDs for all your guests, as this is a legal requirement in the UAE. Kindly share your estimated arrival time to help us ensure a smooth check-in experience. If you have any special preferences during your stay, please let us know in advance so we can make the necessary arrangements. In case you are celebrating a special occasion, our Guest Relations team will be delighted to contact you at your convenience for further details.
